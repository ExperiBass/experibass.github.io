> bio

Bio

I'm a simple fox that makes music, writes code, and speedruns WipEout Pure. I'm usually active on Discord and Matrix.

I've been using JS for about 9 years now, and NodeJS for about 8. Nothing professional sadly, but my open-source projects have been praised before by professionals.

The project I'm proudest of so far is esiJS, a custom-built wrapper for EvE Onlines API.

With my music, I mainly produce Hardstyle/Jumpstyle and Trap, with a bit of Techno sprinkled in. My main inspirations are DJ Coone, Da Tweekaz, and Wildstylez.

I try to blend the 90s to early 2000s style with my own style in my music, and I hope that I convey emotion in each and every one of my releases.

> commissions

Music Commissions

I do take music commissions. I do Hardstyle, Trap, and Techno, and the price ranges from $40 USD to $80 USD (or the equivalent in an accepted crypto) depending on complexity.

If you decide to commission me, I'll need a general mood of your track with reference songs. I do NOT do vocals, though I can use pre-existing vocals (that would bump the price by $25 USD). If you want custom cover art that'll bump the price by whatever the chosen artist to create the covers prices are.

During the creation of your track, I'll send WIPs for your input, and once it's done to your liking and I receive payment I'll send the .mp3 (320 kbps) and .wav.

Contacting

You can contact me via Twitter, Discord (ExperiBassMusic#3335), Matrix (@experibassmusic:minteck.ro.lt) and email (gingkagingerpepper at gmail dot com).

Terms and Conditions

I will not use lewd sounds in my comissions. You can do anything you want to the track once it's finished except resell without my explicit permission. I do reserve the right to use and edit the finished product for myself if I choose.

Payment in fiat will be conducted through Zelle or Venmo.

With crypto payment, the accepted coins are Ethereum, Bitcoin, Monero, Solana, Stellar Lumens, and USD Coin. Other coins may be accepted. Payment in crypto will only be accepted once the transaction gets at least 2 confirmations.

Code Commissions

I also write code on commission. I can do just about anything in JavaScript, including Discord bots and Reddit bots. Other platforms like Telegram can be supported too.

My prices start at a base of $150 USD, and will increase depending on the complexity of the project. Any updates past the original details will have a $50 USD charge.

When I'm commissioned, I require the details to be fully laid out, preferably in a Trello board.

Crypto Payment

If you're paying in crypto, it will only be accepted after 3 to 5 confirmations. Only ETH, XMR, BTC, and LTC are accepted.
